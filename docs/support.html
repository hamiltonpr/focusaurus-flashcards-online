Getting started

  • Guest mode: On the sign-in screen, tap “Continue without account” to use the app without syncing.
  • Sync across devices: Sign in with Google from the sign-in screen or Settings.
  • Import vocabulary: On the Home screen, use Import CSV, or open a stack → Edit → Import from CSV.

Guided reading (Japanese)

  • Guided reading is available for stacks with Japanese text on card fronts.
  • Story library works offline. NHK Easy News requires an internet connection.
  • Tap underlined words in the reader for definitions. Solid underlines are words already in your stack.

Notifications

Optional due-card reminders can be enabled in Settings. iOS and Android will ask for permission the first time you turn them on.

Account & data

  • Sign out: Settings → Account → Sign out.
  • Delete account: Settings → Account → Delete account. This permanently removes your cloud data and cannot be undone.
  • Guest data: Stored only on your device. Uninstalling the app removes local data.

See our Privacy Policy for details on what we collect and how it is used.

Troubleshooting

  • Google sign-in fails: Check your internet connection and try again. If the issue persists, email us with your device model and iOS/Android version.
  • NHK articles won’t load: NHK Easy News requires an active internet connection.
  • Dictionary lookups fail: Tap-to-define uses Jisho.org when online. Check your connection and try again.
  • Sync not updating: Open Settings and confirm you are signed in. Edits save automatically; look for the sync status indicator.
