Delete your account

Focasaurus mobile app · Paige Hamilton

Delete in the app

If you are signed in, you can permanently delete your account from the app:

  1. Open Focasaurus.
  2. Go to Settings (bottom tab).
  3. Scroll to Account.
  4. Tap Delete account and confirm.

Deletion is immediate and cannot be undone.

What gets deleted

  • Your Google-linked sign-in account (authentication record)
  • Cloud-stored flashcard stacks, cards, study progress, and app settings
  • Your email address as stored for your account

What is not deleted automatically

  • Guest mode data is stored only on your device. To remove it, delete your stacks in the app or uninstall Focasaurus.
  • Reading history (story/article IDs) is stored locally on your device only and is not synced to the cloud.

Can’t access the app?

Email paige.rf.hamilton@gmail.com from the Google account email you used to sign in. We will delete your account and cloud data within 30 days.
