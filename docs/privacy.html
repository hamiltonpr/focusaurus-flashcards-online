Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

Focasaurus (“we,” “our,” or “the app”) is operated by Paige Hamilton. This policy explains what information the Focasaurus mobile app collects, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Summary

  • You can use Focasaurus without an account. In guest mode, your stacks stay on your device.
  • If you sign in with Google, we store your flashcard data in the cloud so you can sync across devices.
  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • Some features send limited data to third-party services (described below).

Information we collect

Information you provide

  • Flashcard stacks: stack names, card fronts and backs, study progress, spaced-repetition schedules, per-stack settings, and session statistics.
  • App settings: study goals, notification preferences, and other options you configure in Settings.
  • Reading history: IDs of stories and NHK articles you have read (stored locally on your device only; not synced to the cloud).

Information from sign-in (optional)

If you choose “Sign in with Google,” authentication is handled by Google and Supabase. We receive your Google account email address and a user identifier so we can associate your data with your account. We do not receive your Google password.

Information collected automatically

  • Device notifications: If you enable due-card reminders, the app schedules local notifications on your device. No notification content is sent to our servers.
  • Sync metadata: When signed in, we store a timestamp of when your data was last updated.

How we use your information

  • Provide flashcard study, spaced repetition, and guided reading features.
  • Sync your stacks and settings across devices when you are signed in.
  • Send optional local reminders about due cards, if you enable notifications.
  • Respond to support requests you send us.

We do not use your data for advertising or sell it to third parties.

Where your data is stored

  • Guest mode: Data is stored locally on your device using on-device storage. Uninstalling the app removes this data.
  • Signed-in mode: Your stacks and global settings are stored in a Supabase database (hosted infrastructure). Data is protected by row-level security so only your account can access it.

Third-party services

The app uses the following third-party services when you use related features:

  • Google Sign-In — optional account authentication. Google Privacy Policy
  • Supabase — optional cloud storage and authentication backend. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • Jisho.org — when you tap a word in guided reading while online, the app may send the word to Jisho’s public API to fetch a dictionary definition. We do not control Jisho’s data practices.
  • NHK NEWS WEB EASY — when you read NHK Easy News articles, article content is retrieved through our proxy service. NHK content is subject to NHK’s terms.

These services may process data according to their own privacy policies.

Data retention

  • Guest data remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app.
  • Cloud data is kept while your account is active.
  • Account deletion: You can delete your account from Settings → Delete account. This removes your cloud-stored data and authentication record. Deletion is permanent.

Your choices

  • Use guest mode instead of signing in.
  • Sign out at any time from Settings.
  • Delete your account and cloud data from Settings.
  • Disable notifications in Settings or in your device’s system settings.
  • Avoid online features (NHK Easy News, Jisho lookups) if you prefer not to send data over the internet.

Children’s privacy

Focasaurus is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

Security

We use industry-standard practices including HTTPS for network requests and database access controls. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to protect your data.

International users

If you use the app from outside the United States, your information may be processed in the United States or other countries where our service providers operate.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post the revised version on this page and update the “Last updated” date. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
